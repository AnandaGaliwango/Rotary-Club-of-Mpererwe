Who We Are

The Rotary Club of Mpererwe is part of Rotary International, a global network of 1.4 million neighbors, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change.

Our club brings together business and professional leaders to provide humanitarian service, encourage high ethical standards, and help build goodwill and peace in the world.
